I am a full stack web developer who graduated from Austin Coding Academy in 2022. I was born & raised in New Orleans, Louisiana, where I lived until Hurricane Katrina flooded my house when I was sixteen years old. I attended Loyola University (New Orleans) School of Music Business for one year before deciding to pursue an opportunity to become an insurance adjuster. I worked in insurance for 12 years, traveling often for work & living briefly in places like Bloomington, IL, Dallas, TX & Jacksonville, FL. I also lived in Los Angeles from 2014-2016 & worked at various restaurants in West LA. In 2021, I decided to relocate to Texas & pursue my dream of becoming a software developer. I currently live in the North Houston / Woodlands area, where I would like to stay for the foreseeable future. Austin is my favorite major city to visit, but my overall favorite place to visit is Taos, New Mexico. One of my life goals is to get an RV & travel while working as a remote coder.
